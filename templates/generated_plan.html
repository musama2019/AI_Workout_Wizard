Workout Plan Title: Muscle Gain

            Day 1:
            

                Warm Up (5 minutes):
                
- Light cardio, such as jogging or cycling

                
- Dynamic stretches, such as arm circles and leg swings

            

            

                Workout (volume: 5):
                
- Overhead Press / 3 sets / 8-12 reps / 2 minutes rest / Focus on strict form

                
- Lateral Raises / 3 sets / 10-15 reps / 1 minute rest / Use a weight that challenges you

                
- Reverse Fly / 3 sets / 12-15 reps / 1 minute rest / Keep your lower back straight

                
- Face Pulls / 3 sets / 10-12 reps / 1 minute rest / Emphasize the squeezing motion

            

            

                Cool Down (5 minutes):
                
- Static stretches for the shoulders and chest

            


            Day 2:
            

                Warm Up (5 minutes):
                
- Same as Day 1

            

            

                Workout (volume: 4):
                
- Incline Dumbbell Press / 3 sets / 10-12 reps / 1 minute rest / Maintain a slight arch in your lower back

                
- Dumbbell Fly / 3 sets / 15-20 reps / 1 minute rest / Go to a full stretch at the bottom

                
- Shoulder Press Machine / 3 sets / 10-12 reps / 1 minute rest / Choose a weight that allows you to maintain proper form

            

            

                Cool Down (5 minutes):
                
- Same as Day 1
